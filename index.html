About Riria hub

The Jennifer Riria Hub is an ultra-modern, state of the art facility developed by Echo Network Africa (ENA) formerly Kenya Women Holding (KWH).
The architectural design of the Hub is inspired by the strength, character and gait of the African woman. Located at the junction of Tom Mboya Street and Kipchoge Avenue in Nakuru County.
The Jennifer Riria Hub is strategically positioned to provide a serene environment for its clientele.

Features of the hub

  • Easy access and exit
  • Ample parking
  • 24hrs CCTV surveillance
  • Standby generator
  • Sufficient water supply (County water supply and treated borehole water).
  • Lift services
  • Ample toilet facilities
  • Spacious, airy and natural lighting
  • User friendly to senior citizens and Persons With Disabilities (PWDs)
